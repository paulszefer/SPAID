About SPAID

You may be wondering what the purpose of this SPAID site is. Well, this is your go to site if you are interested in applying to Computer System Technology (CST) at BCIT, or if you are looking for tips and advices from current students as well as working graduates.

This site was created by five CST students from the January intake in 2017. All five of us share the same value of being welcoming to fellow students, you can check out who each individual are in the About Us section. Through this site, we hope you will be able to learn what it is like to be in a wonderful and career shaping program. There are plenty of information and resources presented to engage your interest. Interview stories of current CST students and graduates are also present on this site. You should go check out what they have to say about this program and what tricks and tips they may have to do well in CST! We also offer a social forum space for you guys to bring up any questions you may have that is not answered on our site.

Hopefully SPAID will be able to provide any information that you find valuable for you to take away, and help you get an idea of what CST is like. For those who are already in the program, we hope to help you achieve a healthier, successful and more enjoyable life at BCIT. Enjoy your stay!
