What is CST?

The highly-sought Computer System Technology program at BCIT. It is a two-year diploma program that fuses computer science with practical experience in software development. You’ll learn software engineering and programming from industry professionals, and gain experience working on real projects, from concept to deployment. If you survive first year, in second year specialty options add depth and further refine your skills.

Program Info

There are four terms in the program. For the first year, there are seven courses per term. The work is moderately hard but the volume is immense. You’ll be introduced to core skills and theory used in software development. You’ll also be required to work as a part of a team to design and build an application. Second year is when you pick what specialty you want to go in. Currently there are eight different options for you to specialize in. The number of courses is reduced however the level of work is increased. During second year, you’ll also be engaged with real-world industry projects. Overall, you’re put into sets of about 25 students, and you stay with the same set for the first two terms. Then depending on what specialty, you choose, you’ll be put into a new set. .


Career Opportunities

The CST diploma is a widely-distinguished credential that is highly regarded by all employers. Graduates encompass the well-rounded skills needed for a career analyzing, designing, and developing software solutions. The tech industry is currently booming as every business is looking to get ahead by moving their business into high-tech infrastructure. In British Columbia, there’s 9,700 tech companies and about 86,800 jobs and growing.
