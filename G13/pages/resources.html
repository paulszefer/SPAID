Student Association

The British Columbia Institute of Technology Student Association (BCITSA) is a non-profit student services and advocacy organization for the British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT).

A board of directors composed of 11 elected Student Executive members and Student Councilors from each of the different schools of BCIT governs the affairs of the BCITSA.

An association for the sole purpose of working for BCIT students, BCITSA supports and enhances the quality of student life for all full and part-time students.
